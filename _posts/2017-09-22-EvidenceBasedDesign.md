Our paper *"Evidence-Based Design and Evaluation of a Whole Genome Sequencing Clinical Report for the Reference Microbiology Laboratory"* is now available as a [pre-print](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/early/2017/10/06/199570) .

The goal of this project was to redesign an exisiting TB Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) report by using quantitative and qualitative evidenced gathered by [using human centered design methods](http://www.cs.ubc.ca/labs/imager/tr/2017/MicroReportDesign/). 

I'll also be at [IEEE VIS 2017](http://ieeevis.org/year/2017/info/posters) and [ASM NGS PIPELINES](https://www.asm.org/index.php/upcoming-conferences/2nd-asm-conference-on-rapid-applied-microbial-ngs/item/6317-schedule-at-a-glance) Conferences to talk about this work.
